This is the QWERTY keybinding for 31-edo. The philosophy behind this is that I wanted to keep the major scale on the bottom row while adding microtones atop, with a general rule of getting higher as you go up and right.
It's a bit unintuitive and I'm open to any suggestions for improvement. Right now the graphic (just a jpg, not interactive, sorry) is color-coded to show each note in the major scale and the microtones above it as one color.
